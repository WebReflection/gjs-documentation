GWeather.Timezone

const GWeather = imports.gi.GWeather;

let timezone = new GWeather.Timezone();

A timezone.

Timezones are global to the GWeather world (as obtained by GWeather.location_get_world); they can be gotten by passing GWeather.timezone_get_by_tzid with a tzid like "America/New_York" or "Europe/London".
