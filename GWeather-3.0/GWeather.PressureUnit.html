GWeather.PressureUnit

The measure unit to use for atmospheric pressure values, when retrieved by GWeather.Info.prototype.get_value_pressure.

PressureUnit.INVALID

invalid unit

PressureUnit.DEFAULT

the user preferred pressure unit

PressureUnit.KPA

kiloPascal (* 10^3 Pa)

PressureUnit.HPA

hectoPascal (* 10^2 Pa); also known as millibars, but formatted differently

PressureUnit.MB

millibars; same as GWeather.PressureUnit.hpa

PressureUnit.MM_HG

millimiters of mercury

PressureUnit.INCH_HG

inches of mercury

PressureUnit.ATM

atmospheres
