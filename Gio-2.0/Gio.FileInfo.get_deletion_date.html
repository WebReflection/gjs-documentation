Gio.FileInfo.prototype.get_deletion_date

Returns the GLib.DateTime representing the deletion date of the file, as available in G_FILE_ATTRIBUTE_TRASH_DELETION_DATE. If the G_FILE_ATTRIBUTE_TRASH_DELETION_DATE attribute is unset, null is returned.

Since 2.36

a GLib.DateTime, or null.
